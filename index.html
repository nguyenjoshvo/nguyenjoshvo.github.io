MiOncoCirc

A compendium of circular RNAs compiled from cancer clinical samples at The University of Michigan.

Our studies (v0.1):

  • Castration-Resistant Prostate Cancer (Robinson et al., 2015) (dbGaP Access)
  • Pediatrics Tumors (Mody et al., 2015) (dbGaP Access)
  • Advanced Metastatic Cancers (Robinson et al., 2017) (dbGaP Access)
  • Localized Prostate Cancer (forthcoming)
  • More Advanced Solid Tumors (forthcoming)
  • Multiple Myeloma (forthcoming)
